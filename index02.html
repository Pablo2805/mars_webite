About

ManchesterAlternative RoomS

MARS is a non-profile members club based in Greater Manchester. Money made by events held within Mars, goes back into the running costs and any improvements that come up.

Mars is a venue where people can come to be welcomed into a very friendly environment where people can openly ask questions otherwise would probably be fround upon in other situations.

In the Beginning

Mars sprung to life about 11 years ago after a special woman decided the she could envisage a better environment for people in the Alternative life style.

MARS is mainly made of 5 rooms

  • The Kitchen - This is the heart of the club.
  • The Lounge - This is an area where you can relax, socialize and meet new people.
  • The Medical Room - This is a room where you can play out a medical senerio. complete with a working sink and shower to clean up after the senerio has ended.
  • The Rubber Room - As the name suggests... this is a rubber cladded room where people can be restrained, go on a sensory journey and many other things.
  • The Bunker - This is the main play space which has many unique and unusal pieces of equipment that can be used during any and all events held at MARS. All pieces of equipment can be moved around, put away or taken out depending on the members want to use.

The only limitations that people have during an event at Mars is there imagination and the items they bring.
